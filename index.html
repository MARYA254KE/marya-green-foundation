My Profile

My name is Marya Chepkorir, I'm 14 years old and currently environmental champion of the year by girl up Kenya. I'm a grade 9 student at St. Georges primary school. Away from school, I'm passionate about environment conservation and girls empowerment. I'm championing for climate justice through participation in environment conservation initiatives such as tree planting activities, so far I have planted more than 5000 trees in six schools in Nairobi county and 3 schools outside Nairobi. I'm the current little miss environment Nairobi county and I'm serving as an ambassador of green schools project at youth care Africa. Because of my love for nature, I have managed to mobilize my peers to join me in the campaign against climate change, I'm currently educating my peers on the impacts of climate change to children and how we can mitigate these impacts. Additionally, I'm championing for menstrual equity through Girls Tubonge Initiative where I meet teenage girls and share our knowledge on how we can manage our menstrual flows. I have donated more than 1000 sanitary pads to girls from Kangemi and in Murang'a County and I'm planning for another Girls Tubonge event in Bomet County where I will host more than 300 girls. I'm looking forward to a more sustainable future and a habitable planet
